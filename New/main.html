Computers Top Close Open

I've been using and programming computers for 20-something years now. My work is based around web design and development. I run my own consultancy business specialising in building template-driven web sites like this one.

I'm the author of the Template Toolkit, an Open Source software package written specifically for this purpose. It has become the de-facto templating solution of choice for Perl programmers and is used to build countless web sites from the very small to the very large. I've written a book on the subject and have given a number of talks at the Perl Conference and London.pm user group meetings.

Kites Top Close Open

I'm a freestyle kite flying fanatic. Over the last ten or so years I've flown at numerous kite competitions, festivals and other events around the World. I have designed a number of kites with Tim Benson of Benson Kites, including the Gemini, Airbow and Deep Space. I've also devised a number of freestyle competition formats.

I've appeared in a number of Kite Movies including my home-produced Stoked in the Park series available for download, and the new Trick or Treat DVD from Soul Deep Films, available from all good kite shops.

There is also a section dealing with bridle design and an archive of some of my rec.kites posts or those of other people that I found amusing or interesting
